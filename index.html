Setup ratings reference

A+ The perfect setup
  • Liquidity sweep AND delivery from FVGBoth: sweep and delivery from another FVG where bodies respect it absolutely perfectly
  • Momentum with NO chopStrong momentum through the iFVG, quick reaction to the low/high
  • Targets are clearEQH, EQL, ITH, ITL, Data Highs, Data Lows, or LRLR
  • FVG is singularIf not, check another TF to see if combined they are
  • Premium/DiscountLongs in discount, shorts in premium (SMTs a bonus)
A Strong setup
  • Liquidity sweep OR delivery from FVGEither one, not both
  • MomentumDoesn't need momentum right away like A+, but still good momentum on the break (no chop)
  • Targets are clearOne of the 7
  • FVG is singular
  • Premium/DiscountDelivery FVG must be an obvious FVG in discount (longs) or premium (shorts)
Normal A setups on choppy bad days will be A−
A− / B+ Tradeable
  • Liquidity sweep OR delivery from FVG
  • Momentum with some chopTrade can't take place until the body closes fully over/under the FVG
  • Targets are clear
  • FVG is singular
  • Premium/Discount may be offLongs may be in premium, shorts may be in discount
B+ = an A− on a very choppy/bad day where the gap itself looks super obvious but conditions aren't amazing
B Risky
  • No sweep and/or no deliveryNot delivering from anywhere, no sweep — may go along with bias still
  • Momentum with a lot of chop
  • Target exists but unlikely to have multiple confluences
  • Singular FVG may not be obvious
  • Longs in premium / shorts in discount
B− Avoid
  • No major sweep or deliveryWorks sometimes if bias is right
  • Low momentum with chopBad conditions or low volume
  • No real targetsR:R sucks
  • FVG won't be obviousMessy PA, violating inverses back up and down
C Do not trade
  • No sweep, no deliveryTrading in the middle of nowhere — does not make sense to take
  • Momentum unpredictable
  • No targets, no biasSomething else is holding price that makes the setup irrelevant
  • Forcing an FVG just to take a trade
Golden rule: A− / B+ and above = tradeable. B and below = stand down. The best trade you'll take this week might be the one you skip.
